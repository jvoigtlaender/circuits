Works best in Firefox.

  • Click in inventory at the right to put new tiles on the plate.
  • Drag tiles around using the mouse.
  • Rotate tiles by clicking on them.
  • Make wire connections between tiles by clicking on the knobs.
  • Delete wires/tiles by holding a tile and pressing 'Del' key.
  • Operate switches by pressing corresponding keys '0', '1', '2', ...
